My career profile is a personalised career exploration tool that takes you through several activities to help you build a career profile, explore career suggestions, and create a career plan. Once you have completed either the Interests or Skills activity, My career profile will start to show you occupation suggestions based on your career profile. As you do more activities, the detail in your profile increases and the selection of occupations will match your career profile more closely.

As a Guest user you can explore My career profile and discover occupations that may interest you. However by creating an account, you will be registered and able to save your career profile to revisit at any time.
